## 完整检查工作流更新时序图

sequenceDiagram

    Note over WorkflowService: Compare request.rulesHashes.ruleIds<br/>with workflow.orderedSteps sequence

4. **性能优化**: 条件批量查询规则，避免N+1查询问题
5. **空值处理**: rulesHashes为空时直接返回空的更新列表
